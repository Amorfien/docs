Privacy Policy for xStitch

Last updated: 10/18/25

Introduction

xStitch ("we," "our," or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

Information Collection and Use

The xStitch app does not collect, store, or process any personal data from users. We do not require registration accounts, and we do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers.

Advertising

xStitch uses Yandex Ads to display advertisements. Yandex Ads may collect certain non-personal information for ad targeting purposes, such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • General location data (non-precise)
  • Advertising IDs

For more information about Yandex Ads privacy practices, please refer to Yandex's Privacy Policy: https://yandex.com/legal/confidential/

Third-Party Services

The app uses Yandex Ads as a third-party service. These services have their own privacy policies governing data collection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
